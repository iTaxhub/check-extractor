```
Cheque Extractor: Automate Your Cheque Processing Workflow

Transform the way you handle cheques with Cheque Extractor, the intelligent OCR solution designed specifically for accounting professionals and businesses using QuickBooks Online.

WHAT IS CHEQUE EXTRACTOR?

Cheque Extractor is an advanced accounting automation tool that uses Optical Character Recognition (OCR) and Artificial Intelligence to extract structured data from cheque images and seamlessly integrate it with your QuickBooks Online account. Say goodbye to manual data entry and hello to accuracy and efficiency.

KEY FEATURES:

• Intelligent OCR Extraction
  - Automatically extract payee names, amounts, dates, cheque numbers, and memo lines
  - Advanced AI recognizes both printed and handwritten text
  - Confidence scores help you identify extractions that need review
  - Support for various cheque formats and layouts

• Direct QuickBooks Integration
  - One-click sync to QuickBooks Online
  - Create Expense or Check transactions automatically
  - Intelligent duplicate detection prevents double-entry
  - Automatic vendor mapping and categorization

• Smart Reconciliation
  - Compare uploaded cheques with existing QuickBooks transactions
  - Identify matches and discrepancies instantly
  - Side-by-side comparison view for easy verification
  - Bulk reconciliation for multiple cheques

• Batch Processing
  - Upload and process multiple cheques simultaneously
  - Queue management for efficient workflow
  - Progress tracking for large batches
  - Export results in multiple formats

• Data Validation & Review
  - Edit extracted data before syncing to QuickBooks
  - Field-level validation ensures data accuracy
  - Visual confirmation with original cheque image
  - Audit trail for all changes

• Secure & Compliant
  - Bank-level encryption (TLS 1.3 + AES-256)
  - SOC 2 Type II compliant infrastructure
  - Automatic data backups
  - User-controlled data retention

PERFECT FOR:

✓ Accounting Firms
  - Process client cheques efficiently
  - Reduce data entry time by up to 90%
  - Improve accuracy and reduce errors
  - Scale your practice without adding staff

HOW IT WORKS:

1. Upload Cheque Images
   - Take photos with your phone or scan cheques
   - Drag and drop multiple images at once
   - Supports JPG, PNG, and PDF formats

2. AI Extracts Data
   - Advanced OCR processes each cheque
   - AI identifies and structures all relevant fields
   - Confidence scores indicate extraction quality

3. Review & Edit
   - Verify extracted data against original image
   - Edit any fields as needed
   - Add notes or additional information

4. Sync to QuickBooks
   - One-click sync to your QuickBooks account
   - Choose transaction type (Expense or Check)
   - Automatic duplicate detection
   - Instant confirmation

5. Reconcile & Report
   - Compare with existing QuickBooks transactions
   - Identify matches and discrepancies
   - Generate reconciliation reports
   - Export for record-keeping
```
